The Public House is a full bar situated conveniently in the Collegiate Plaza on the corner of Alafaya and University in Orlando, FL.

 

The Public House is 21 and up at all times.

 

The Public House boasts a selection of 24 draughts and a full liquor bar, in addition to many wines and craft beers.

 

Proudly serving Orlando since 2013!
